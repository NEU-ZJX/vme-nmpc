Calculate gradient from ∂J = ∑∂H/∂u ∂u, then step the control set.

Get the gradient ∂H/∂u_k, for each step, k in the horizon, loop through each k in N. This involves computing the obstacle potential and Lagrange multipliers. Then, the control plan is updated by stepping against the direction of the gradient.
